Phase 3.5C: Complete Rename Table

Naming rules applied

1. I → Intensity everywhere (e.g., atmI → atmosphereIntensity)
2. Tone stays Tone — already spelled out
3. Alpha stays Alpha — already spelled out
4. HueSlot stays HueSlot — already spelled out
5. Abbreviations expanded: bg → background, fg → nothing (use element context), atm → atmosphere, txt → contentText, I suffix → Intensity
6. Context aligns with 3.5B vocabulary: fg prefix becomes element or specific text type; bg becomes surface; atm becomes atmosphere
